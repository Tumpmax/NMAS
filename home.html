Our Guiding Scripture

Hebrews 13:1-4 - “Real love for others produces tangible actions”


Four actions based on Hebrews 13:1-4

1. Kindness to strangers 2. Empathy for those who are in prison and those who have been mistreated 3. Respect for your marriage vows 4. Contentment with what you have

Our Mission Statement

Never Meet a Stranger Ministry provides purpose and tools for everyone to succeed in all areas of life. We provide access to resources in a variety of ways, including but not limited to, events, referral lists, and one-on-one mentoring. Our desire is for people to believe in themselves and to be confident, which will allow them to stand out and to know they are never alone.
