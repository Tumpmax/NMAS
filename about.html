Never Meet A Stranger Ministry, Inc.


Our History

NMAS was started by Shantrell Otummkpo on 3/10/2020 and her foundational goal was to assist women in realizing their God-given talents and gifts. NMAS was birthed from a strong desire to help others by creating an atmosphere where God’s love, strength, and peace resides - which will facilitate mental, emotional, and spiritual growth.
